Our story

PingPongNinja was developed and designed by James Dennes, and Dave Martin out of Sydney Australia. James and Dave both work at CampaingMonitor.com. Initially just an internal app used by the crew at Campaign Monitor, James and Dave thought, "Hey, a lot of companies would probably enjoy this". So they set off to develop a version that could host multiple accounts.

The app is hosted through Google App Engine and relies on the app-engine-patch and jQuery for it's functionality.
